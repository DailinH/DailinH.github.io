Short Bio

I am a first year Computer Science Ph.D. student in the Donald Bren School of Information & Computer Science at UC Irvine. My faculty advisor is Roy Fox and I’m a member of the Intelligent Dynamics Lab. I was an exchange student enrolled under the EECS department at UC Irvine in my senior year. I received my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Southeast University, China.

Research Interests

My current research interest mainly involves theory and applications of reinforcement learning in artificial intelligence. My undergraduate research touches upon the optimization of large-scale multi-agent reinforcement learning.
