Research Interests

My research interests main lies in Combinatorial optimization and algorithms, and my prior research experiences touch upon theory and applications of Reinforcement Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Previously, I worked on the optimization of large-scale multi-agent reinforcement learning as an undergraduate research assistant for Prof. W. Wu in Southeast University, China. In large-scale multi-agent systems(MASs), the search costs of traditional centralized RL methods usually grow exponentially with the number of agents. Image-based state representation methods, on the other hand, do not take such exponential cost, but show limited performance. We proposed a framework with hierarchically organized manager-worker modules that breaks a large-scale problem into sub-problems and shows desirable performance.
